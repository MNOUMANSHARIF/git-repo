Mustang GT

If it's horsepower you're after, the Mustang GT is the one to get. The pony car packs a 5.0-liter V8 good for 460 horsepower and 420 pound-feet, and comes with
the choice of either a slick six-speed manual transmission or a 10-speed automatic. Standard features include things like launch control, 18-inch wheels,
and the latest Ford Sync infotainment system. The Mustang GT costs just $35,630 to start.
