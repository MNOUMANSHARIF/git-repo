WELCOME TO UoCP


A warm welcome from the faculty, staff, and administration of the University of Central Pakistan! The University was founded to provide students a solid education that focuses on providing vision and viable solutions for business, government, and industry. UoCP is here to help our students prepare for a successful career. We accomplish this by providing many unique features that are embedded in our rigorous curricula, including strong international emphasis and comprehensive integration of theory and practical experience. At UoCPstudents have the opportunity to explore the globalizing marketplace while honing their cultural, historical, and social acumen with which they are enabled to excel in business and technology. These features distinguish UoNA education and equip our graduates with the knowledge and skills necessary to become leaders in this ever-changing world. The University's motto is "Education That Transforms," and that is our goal for each student. We urge our students to take advantage of the programs and resources that are specifically developed for them. Meanwhile, we cordially extend our invitation to everyone who is seeking an affordable quality education to browse through our website or visit our campus to learn more about our institution. Please contact us if you have any questions or to ask for an appointment with our staff. Our campus is open to you.
