Mustang EcoBoost

The cheapest way to get into a new Mustang is by opting for the base EcoBoost model. Powered by a turbocharged 2.3-liter four-cylinder engine,
the entry-level pony car produces 310 horsepower and 350 pound-feet of torque, which means it can sprint to 60 miles per hour in a sprightly 4.9 seconds.
The base Mustang EcoBoost fastback costs $26,670, while the convertible costs $32,170.
